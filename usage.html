Using my music

Terms of usage

All my music can be used in your videos on Youtube, Facebook and Instagram. You can even monetize your videos on youtube. But to be able to use my music you need to agree with the terms below and follow the steps:

  • You must give me credit when using my music (Example below)
  • You can not make claims that my music is yours
  • If you use my music outside YouTube, Facebook or Instagram you need to contact me in order to get a special permission or license

Using my song Adventures

If you want to use my song "Adventures" outside of the popular social media and streaming platforms you need to get in contact with Argofox. https://www.youtube.com/c/ArgofoxCC/about

Copyright claims on YouTube

There have been cases where content creators that are using my music in their videos get copyright claimed. It can be random people claiming the videos or from third party services where people are fraudently releasing my music and opting into Content ID. The solution is to simply dispute it and letting me know who made the claim so I can report it.
